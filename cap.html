Your Question

Why CAP ?

  • To promote E-cell RGPV in your college .
  • To organize events and meetups .
  • To connect with other ambassadors and share resources.
  • To promote,conduct and cooperate in the promotion of activities of a professional, educational, cultural and social nature amongst the students .

What CAP has planned for you ?

  • You will gain Exposure and personal connections.
  • Certificate on successful completion of the program.
  • Most active ambassadors on 25+ registrations from their side will get goodies & hampers and free entry in the events while on 45+ registrations they will get direct entry in the interview round .
  • This will Help you to learn and explore new technical dimensions and to develop skills
  • You gain experience working as a part of a marketing team.
  • You will improve your communication and marketing abilities with the various tasks given to campus ambassadors.

Who can apply ?

Students having strong leadership and immense love and interest for entrepreneurship, are most welcome to be a part of their extended family on their university.

About CAP

e-Entrepreneurship Cell is a non-profit organisation solely with the purpose of creating awareness towards the developing entrepreneurial culture in our surrounding and encourage students to embrace the idea of starting their own venture and also to inspire and guide young entrepreneurs and their start-ups.


The campus ambassador program is an initiative by E-Cell RGPV. To incorporate entrepreneurial spirit in students, it is a platform for the students who are passionate about entrepreneurship to start and manage E-cell RGPVin their college . A CAP member will be a face,voice and evangelist of E-Cell RGPV at their college or university and will be responsible for hosting events and building a large network and to promote E-Cell RGPV in college.The objective of this program ranges from social networking to enhancement of knowledge of the members of this program, including various other benefits that shall come through in the course of the membership of CAP.
