ECELL

e-Entrepreneurship Cell is a non-profit organisation solely with the purpose of creating awareness towards the developing entrepreneurial culture in our surrounding and encourage students to embrace the idea of starting their own venture and also to inspire and guide young entrepreneurs and their start-ups.

About RGPV

An initiative of Rajiv Gandhi Proudyogiki Vishwavidhyalaya, Bhopal to promote the idea of Entrepreneurship. The e-Entrepreneurship Cell is established by RGPV to create the awareness among the students about the Entrepreneurial Culture emerging in the nation

UIT-RGPV

The initiative is supported by the constituent institution University Institute of Technology - Rajiv Gandhi Proudyogiki Vishwavidhyalaya, Bhopal to promote the idea of Entrepreneurship
